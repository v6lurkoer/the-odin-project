Fried potatoes

Description

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (not mash potatoes)
  • Canola oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: potato seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Add oil and make sure it covers the entire pan
  3. Add potatoes, shake to spread
  4. Shake after one minute, repeat two or three times
  5. Add pinch of salt
