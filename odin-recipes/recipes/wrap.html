Wrap

Description

Ingredients

  • Beans of your choosing
  • Two vegetables of your choosing (broccoli and bell pepper recommended)
  • Soy mince/tofu/seitan
  • Cream sauce
  • (Corn) Tortilla

Steps

  1. Prepare the protein (with teriyaki sauce)
  2. Boil beans
  3. Add cream sauce on tortilla
  4. Add beans, vegetables and protein on the tortilla
  5. Roll it up
